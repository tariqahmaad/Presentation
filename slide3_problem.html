Problem Analysis & Research Background

The Core Problem

Fragmented Management

Users juggle 3-5 apps for basic money management, leading to incomplete visibility.

Manual Data Overload

70% abandon expense tracking within 3 months due to manual categorization.

No Intelligent Insights

Basic reports exist but lack AI-driven recommendations for financial behavior.

Social Expense Chaos

Shared expenses rely on spreadsheets and memory - causing relationship stress.

Why This Matters

Traditional tools create high barriers to entry for everyday users.

Manual tracking leads to data fragmentation and abandonment of financial goals.

Users need intelligent automation that eliminates manual categorization burden.

Why Current Solutions Fail

Complexity Over Usability

Apps prioritize features over user experience, creating usage barriers.

Lack of Intelligence

No solution combines AI categorization, social tracking, and insights in one platform.

Platform Fragmentation

Multiple apps needed for financial management create data silos and friction.
