Problem Statement & Market Gap

The Core Problem

Fragmented Financial Management

Users juggle 3-5 different apps for basic money management, leading to incomplete financial visibility.

Manual Data Overload

70% of users abandon expense tracking within 3 months due to time-consuming manual categorization.

No Intelligent Insights

Existing tools provide basic reports but lack AI-driven recommendations for improving financial behavior.

Social Expense Chaos

Shared expenses and debt tracking rely on spreadsheets, messages, and memory - causing relationship stress.

Market Impact & Statistics

64% of adults lack confidence in their financial decisions due to poor tracking tools.

Personal finance app market is $1.2B+ but lacks AI-first solutions for comprehensive management.

85% of young adults want real-time financial insights but current apps provide only basic historical reports.

Why Current Solutions Fail

Complexity Over Usability

Existing apps prioritize features over user experience, creating barriers to consistent usage.

Lack of Intelligence

No current solution combines AI categorization, social expense tracking, and personalized insights in one platform.

Platform Fragmentation

Users need multiple apps for different aspects of financial management, creating data silos and workflow friction.
