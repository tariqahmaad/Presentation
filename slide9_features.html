System Features

  • Three account types: Balance, Income, Savings Goal
  • Data-safe design preventing accidental deletion

  • Auto-sorting by urgency (Overdue, Due Today)

  • Cloud-synchronized preferences across devices

5. AI Chat Interface

  • Natural language financial questions
  • Personalized insights from spending data
  • AI-suggested questions for financial discovery
